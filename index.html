Tanawha Trail, Blue Ridge Parkway

A Walk in the Woods

The Tanawha Trail runs 13 miles one-way, tracing the Blue Ridge Parkway. Rated as moderate, the Tanawha trails passes by lakes and creeks, making it a popular trail for hikers, runners and fisherfolk alike.
